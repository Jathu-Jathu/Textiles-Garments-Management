FAQ


  • HAVE YOU RECEIVED MY RETURN?

    Please contact us via our contact us form on our website with all mandatory details (Name, Email, Telephone) & your Order Number. Alternatively email us with the same details. We will let you know if your returns were received to our head office.

  • WHAT HAPPENS IF I AM NOT AVAILABLE WHEN MY ORDER ARRIVES?

    Our delivery partner will only attempt delivery after contacting you prior to delivery to ensure that you are on the premises when the parcel is delivered to you. If you are not in due to an unforeseen circumstance, you will need to pick up from our delivery partner’s office in your location.

  • HOW DO I CANCEL AN ORDER?

    If you wish to cancel an order, please contact us via within 2 hours of placing the order. Please fill in all fields (Name, Email, Telephone) & tell us why you would like to cancel the order. Ensure to input your Order Number & Date of Purchase in the comments section so we can track your order. Alternatively email us with the same details.

  • WHAT DO I DO IF MY ORDER HAS NOT ARRIVED?

    If your order has not arrived within 7 days please get in touch with us through our contact us form or email us. We will see that our delivery specialists locate it. On confirmation of its location, we will contact you with details of when the delivery can be expected. In the instance that the order has not been dispatched or received, you will have the option of cancelling the order.

  • WHAT DO I DO IF THERE IS AN ERROR IN MY REFUND?

    We are very sorry if we have made a mistake on your refund. Please get in touch with us through our contact us form or email us and we will sort it out for you as soon as possible.
